Two algorithms for thinning binarized images

The idea is to abrade a binarized image until only the stable skeleton is left. We implement two algos from the 90's with WebGL.

  • A fast parallel algorithm for thinning digital patterns (1984), by T.Y. Zhang and C.Y. Suen.
  • Parallel thinning with two sub-iteration algorithms (1989), by Zicheng Guo and Richard Hall.
  • Useful: survey of four algorithms.

To launch the thinning, select anything else than none in the drop-down menu. Or . A funny fact is that if you invert the source image, you'll get a complementary skeleton that meshes perfectly with the normal one.
